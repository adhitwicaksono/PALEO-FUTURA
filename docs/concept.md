# PALEO-FUTURA Concept

PALEO-FUTURA is a conceptual and computational framework for studying evolution as a time-spanning process.

- **PALEO** infers plausible ancestral biological states.
- **FUTURA** forecasts plausible future evolutionary responses under defined scenarios.

The framework does not aim to resurrect extinct organisms or predict exact future genomes. Instead, it produces testable hypotheses about genes, pathways, regulatory features, and evolutionary pressures.

## Core idea

Genomes can be treated as evolutionary archives and evolving systems.

PALEO looks backward into evolutionary memory.

FUTURA looks forward into evolutionary possibility.

Together, they help ask:

> How did this biological system become what it is, and how might it respond to future pressures?

## Main scientific applications

- plant stress adaptation
- crop resilience
- conservation genomics
- regulatory evolution
- synthetic biology design
- space biology
- evolutionary risk assessment
- gene-family evolution in non-model organisms

## Key rule

All predictions must include uncertainty and recommended validation.
